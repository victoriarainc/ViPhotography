About Vi

Creator. Artist. Raleighite.

Candid

ViPhotography focuses on you. As a photographer, Vi uses experience to elicit your personality in every shot. As a person she takes the time to connect to every customer, find your most prevailing qualities, and bring them to the forefront of the picture.

Natural

Modern trends in both social media and marketing have moved towards using broad daylight rather than bright flourescents that creates a harsh light. ViPhotography strictly uses light available during the time of a shoot in order to achieve a truer image.

Genuine

With minimal editing equals faster turn around times and lower prices for you! Staying true to an authentic representation of you is ViPhotography's first priority. In post-production, your photos are enhanced just enough to highlight your best side - not distort who you are.

Flexible
